Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I get an appointment?

We typically have availability within 2-3 business days. For urgent consultations, we offer same-day appointments subject to counselor availability.

What if I need to reschedule?

You can reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours in advance without any charges. Less than 24 hours notice may incur a rescheduling fee.

Are online consultations as effective as in-person?

Yes! Our online consultations are just as effective. We use professional video conferencing tools and provide the same quality of guidance and materials.

What should I prepare for the consultation?

Come with your academic records, any career assessments you've taken, and a list of questions or concerns. If you've taken our quiz, we'll review those results together.

Do you offer group consultations?

Yes, we offer group consultations for families or friends exploring career options together. Group rates are available - contact us for details.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your consultation, we'll provide a full refund or complimentary follow-up session.
