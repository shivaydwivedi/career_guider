Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are typically addressed within 2-4 hours.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure our counselors are available and can dedicate proper time to your needs.

Do you provide services outside of India?

Currently, our primary focus is on Indian students and the Indian education system. However, we do offer online consultations for international students interested in Indian institutions.

What languages do you support?

We provide services in Hindi, English, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, and Bengali. Our counselors can accommodate various regional languages based on availability.

Is there a charge for initial consultations?

We offer a free 15-minute initial consultation call to understand your needs and explain our services. Detailed consultations are part of our paid packages.

How do I track my inquiry status?

After submitting your inquiry, you'll receive a confirmation email with a ticket number. You can use this to track your inquiry status by calling our helpline.
